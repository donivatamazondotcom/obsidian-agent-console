Agent Console puts an AI agent right next to your notes. Here are a few things people use it for.

## Ideas to try

- **Turn a rough outline into a draft.** Open a note, start a chat, and ask the agent to expand your bullet points into prose.
- **Interrogate your own vault.** Ask about the note you're in — the agent reads it as context and answers in place.
- **Run parallel trains of thought.** Keep one tab digging into research while another drafts the summary. Each tab is its own session.
- **Refactor and review code** without leaving Obsidian — paste a file, ask for a cleanup, get a diff back.
- **Keep a working log.** Let the agent capture decisions and action items as you talk a problem through.

## How it works

Pick an agent, open a chat in the sidebar, and type. Mention any note with `@` to pull it into the conversation. Open as many session tabs as you need — they all stick around across restarts.
